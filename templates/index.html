Estimating the Environmental Impact of Travel

This tool provides:

  1. An estimate of air traffic (direct flights) between two cities.
  2. A prediction of future air traffic between the cities, until 2025.
  3. The estimated total carbon dioxide emissions by flights along the route, and a per person estimate.
  4. The carbon dioxide emission estimates for other modes of transport, as a table.

Simply enter the origin and destination cities (and state if necessary).
